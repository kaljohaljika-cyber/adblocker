Common checks

  • Keep your recovery key private. It is required to recover access to the account.
  • If Android asks for VPN approval, approve only when you intended to connect.
  • If you have no VPN minutes left, earn or receive more minutes before connecting.
  • If App Tunnel is enabled, choose the target apps before connecting.
  • If installing a direct APK update, Android may ask you to allow installs from 0x VPN.
